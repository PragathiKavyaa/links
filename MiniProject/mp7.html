Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 Teaspoon - Turmeric Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon - Jeera Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon - Coriander Powder
  • 2 Teaspoon - Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 ½ Teaspoon - Red Chilli Powder
  • ½ Teaspoon - Garam Masala Powder
  • ½ Teaspoon - Kasoori Methi
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon - Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon - Jeera
  • 1 Pc Cinnamon
  • 1 or 2 Elaichi
  • 1 - Fine Chopped Onion
  • 4 - 5 Tomatoes
  • 3 Tablespoon - Butter
  • 250 gms - Paneer Cubes
  • Fine chopped coriander

Instructions

  1. Heat a little oil and add Jeera, a piece cinnamon, 1 or 2 Elaichi & ginger garlic paste
  2. Add finely chopped onions and fry it till the onions turn to golden brown
  3. Add turmeric powder, jeera powder, Coriander powder, red chilli powder and garam masala powder
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, and salt. Cook till the raw smell disappears
  5. Then add little water and allow it to boil well
  6. Then add the paneer cubes and mix well.
  7. Add Kasoori methi, butter and garnish with chopped coriander
